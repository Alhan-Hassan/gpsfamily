The Story of Our Charity Day

On this special day, we came together as God's Prayer Squad to make a tangible difference in our community. We believe that prayer, when combined with action, can create miracles. Our annual Charity Day is a testament to this belief, bringing us closer to those in need and allowing us to be the hands and feet of our faith.

This year, our focus was on providing essential resources to homeless families in the city center. We organized a food drive, a clothing collection, and a mobile clinic offering basic health check-ups. The outpouring of support from volunteers and donors was truly heartwarming and a powerful display of community spirit. Over 200 families were served, with more than 500 meals distributed and countless clothing items donated.

Our mission extends beyond immediate aid. We aim to foster hope, dignity, and connection through our outreach programs. By partnering with local organizations, we ensure that our efforts have a lasting impact, helping families transition to stability with ongoing support.
