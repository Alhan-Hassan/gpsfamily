Our Mission

Our mission is to spread God's love through a dedicated ministry of prayer and compassionate service.

We exist to build a foundation of spiritual strength through prayer, which empowers our outreach efforts to those in need.

Our calling is to demonstrate the power of God's word by putting our faith into tangible service for our neighbors.

Check out our timetable to find a session that fits your schedule and come experience the power of the GPS Family. We are always happy to welcome new members into our fold.
