The History of God's Prayer Squad (GPS): A Journey of Faith and Prayer

What is GPS (GOD'S PRAYER SQUAD)? It is a family which, by the grace of GOD, was established by GOD to enable every born-again Christian to live a life of prayer, constantly praying with the true revelation of who they are in Christ. The goal of GPS is also to ensure unity in the body of Christ. It is a family of believers from various denominations who are dedicated to intercession and to reaching all people, as well as the body of Christ, with the spirit of prayer.

GOD'S PRAYER SQUAD (GPS)
We do not promote any denomination or sectarianism.
It is not under any denomination. People from all denominations are members of GPS.
PRAYER is our life. When you are with us, expect to pray a lot with the correct revelations** through the Word of GOD.
    BENEFITS OF JOINING THE GPS FAMILY
  • To know your purpose.
  • To learn to pray with correct revelations.
  • To be taught the true love of Christ.
  • To receive sound revelation teachings.

GPS's history is a testament to prayer's power, transforming lives and communities one petition at a time. Join the squad to experience divine guidance today!
