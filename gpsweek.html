About GPS Week

GPS Week is a sacred week-long event where God's Prayer Squad (GPS) members unite for fasting, prayer, and fellowship. The week concludes with a vibrant final day of celebration, filled with worship, testimonies, and community activities, reflecting the joy of unity in faith.

The Week of Fasting and Prayer

During the first six days, GPS members engage in fasting and prayer, focusing on global peace, unity in associations, and spiritual growth. Guided by Isaiah 58:6, they seek to "loose the chains of injustice" through daily prayer themes, including national revival and community outreach, fostering a deeper connection with God and each other.

The Final Day of Celebration

The final day, September 22, 2025, is a joyous gathering at Tafesi Chapel with worship services, shared testimonies, fun games, and a community meal. Inspired by Psalm 133:1, members celebrate the unity and answered prayers of GPS Week, strengthening their bonds as a prayerful community.
